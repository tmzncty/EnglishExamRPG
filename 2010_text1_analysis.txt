2010年 Text 1
文章主题：报纸艺术评论的衰落 (Journalism / Arts)

【词汇注释】
1. inexorable [ɪnˈeksərəbl] adj. 不可阻挡的；无法改变的
   context_match: inexorable decline (不可遏止的衰落)
2. coverage [ˈkʌvərɪdʒ] n. 新闻报道
   context_match: arts coverage (艺术报道)
3. scope [skəʊp] n. 范围
4. seriousness [ˈsɪəriəsnəs] n. 严肃性

【长难句分析】
Source 41: It is difficult to the point of impossibility for the average reader under the age of forty to imagine a time when high-quality arts criticism could be found in most big-city newspapers.
结构分析：It (形式主语) is difficult ... for ... to imagine (真正主语) ...
译文：对于40岁以下的普通读者来说，很难想象在大多数大城市报纸上能找到高质量艺术评论的时代。

【题目解析】
21. It is indicated in Paragraphs 1 and 2 that
[A] arts criticism has disappeared from big-city newspapers
[B] English-language newspapers used to carry more arts reviews
[C] high-quality newspapers retain a large body of readers
[D] young readers doubt the suitability of criticism on dailies

正确答案：[B]
命题解密 (analysis_raw)：
可见,英文报纸过去常刊载更多的艺术评论,[B]正确。
[A]断章取义。文章第一段提到 "inexorable decline" (不可遏止的衰落)，艺术评论只是 decline 而非 disappear (消亡)，选项夸大其词。
[C]错误推出。文中未提及高质量报纸保留了大量读者。
[D]无中生有。文中提到年轻读者很难想象那个时代，但没说他们怀疑评论的适用性。
