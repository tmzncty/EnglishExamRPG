# AI Image Generation Prompts

Here are the prompts you can use with tools like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E 3 to generate the assets for your English Exam RPG.

## 1. Character: The Anime Teacher ("Sensei")

**Style:** Anime style, high quality, cel shaded, visual novel sprite.

**Appearance:**
- **Age:** Late 20s.
- **Outfit:** Professional but stylish teacher attire. White blouse, pencil skirt (navy or black), glasses, maybe a cardigan draped over shoulders.
- **Expression:** Strict but caring.
- **Pose:** Standing straight, holding a pointer stick or a book.

**Prompts:**

*   **Neutral/Happy:** `anime teacher character sprite, female, late 20s, wearing glasses and professional teacher outfit, white blouse, navy pencil skirt, holding a book, white background, visual novel style, high quality, cel shaded, friendly smile --ar 2:3`
*   **Strict/Angry:** `anime teacher character sprite, female, late 20s, wearing glasses, professional outfit, holding a pointer stick, angry expression, stern look, pointing at viewer, white background, visual novel style, high quality, cel shaded --ar 2:3`
*   **Surprised/Impressed:** `anime teacher character sprite, female, late 20s, wearing glasses, professional outfit, surprised expression, adjusting glasses, impressed look, white background, visual novel style, high quality, cel shaded --ar 2:3`

## 2. Backgrounds

**Locations:**
